Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni (optional)
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives (optional)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Olive oil, for brushing
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Place the dough on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  6. Add your toppings such as pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, and olives.
  7. Sprinkle dried oregano and basil over the toppings.
  8. Lightly brush the crust edges with olive oil for a golden finish.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool for 5 minutes.
  11. Slice and serve hot. Enjoy your homemade pizza!
